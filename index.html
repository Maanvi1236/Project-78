What Is The Use Of Console Screen

The console is an operating system window where users interact with the operating system or with a text-based console application by entering text input through the computer keyboard, and by reading text output from the computer terminal.

How To Open Console Screen On Google Chrome

Step 1 - To open the console in Chrome, use this keyboard shortcut: Cmd + Option + J (on a Mac) or Ctrl +Shift +J (on Windows).

OR

Step 1 - You can right-click on the webpage and click "Inspect" to open the developer console.

Step 2 - Click the "Console" tab in that window.

How To Print Anything On Console Screen.
